About Us

Aroma Power Raise Private Limited is a premier aroma oil manufacturing and cultivating company specializing in the production of high-quality aroma oils derived from various plants, herbs, and flowers. Our oils are used in a wide range of applications, including aromatherapy, pharma, food processing cosmetics, personal care, and household products.

Our Expertise

Cultivation

We cultivate a diverse range of aromatic plants including PALMAROSA, JASMINE SAMBAC, GRANDIFLORUM, CITRONELLA, CARDAMOM, PEPPER, DHAVANAM, SWEET BASIL, and VETIVER using sustainable and organic farming practices.

Extraction

Our expert team employs various extraction methods including steam distillation, cold pressing, and solvent extraction to preserve the pure essence of each plant.

Quality Control

We maintain rigorous quality standards through comprehensive testing of chemical composition, purity, and potency.

Distribution

Our products reach global markets through retail stores, online platforms, and wholesale distribution channels.
